# Product Brief

## What It Is
ascii-buddy is a terminal Tamagotchi-like companion that lives in your CLI as animated ASCII art, speaks developer wisdom generated by AI, and persists between sessions — so your buddy is always waiting for you when you open a new terminal.

## Who It's For
Solo developers and terminal power users who spend most of their day in the CLI. Their main goal is a touch of personality and humor in an otherwise sterile environment — a tiny creature that knows their craft and calls them out when they've been staring at the same bug for three hours.

## The Problem It Solves
Terminal work is isolated and monotonous. Developers lack a lightweight, fun, personality-driven companion that speaks their language (literally — dev humor, jargon, sarcasm) without leaving the terminal or requiring a browser tab.

## Key Features
- Animated ASCII companion with mood states (idle, happy, sad, talking, sleeping)
- AI-generated dev phrases tailored to the buddy's personality and talent
- Persistent storage — buddy survives terminal restarts
- Multiple buddy support with an interactive TUI picker (`--list` flag)
- Pluggable AI backend (starts with Gemini, domain-abstracted for easy swap)

## Business Objective
Reach 500 GitHub stars within 3 months of release and maintain a sub-3s startup time so it can be added to any shell profile without friction.

## Out of Scope
- GUI or web interface of any kind
- Multiplayer / shared buddies
- Buddy evolution mechanics or inventory systems (v1)
- Mobile or non-terminal platforms
